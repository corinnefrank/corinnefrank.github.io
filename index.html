About ME

I’m a Utah transplant from the Pacific Northwest. I’ve spent the last few years re-discovering my passions and learning about what I love: hiking with my dogs, paddle boarding, camping with my family, and playing the piano.  This summer will be all about learning how to backpack and getting as much fresh air as possible.

I’m also a nerd who is committed to life-long learning that takes an interest in most subjects and I enjoy reading both fiction and non-fiction.

In design, I’m continually building skills in Branding and Logo Design as well as Figma and Adobe Creative suite.  In the studio I’m exploring abstract art with a focus on mark making for personal enrichment and surface pattern design with the intent to license commercially.

Interests

My design interests are to create cohesive digital products that are thoughtful, beautiful, and intuitive.  

I love understated interactions in hover states such as simple animations or color changes.  I believe that good design can stand on it’s own and that too many noisy interactions, videos, colors, etc. can be a detriment to the flow and usability of a page.

I would be interested in working with companies who have strong core values and contribute to the enrichment and well being of the environment and/or improving the quality of life such as: recreational companies, travel industry, arts, companies that further education or help the disadvantaged, and so on.
